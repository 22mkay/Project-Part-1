Project Overview:
This website is about an online sneaker retailer, that sells high quality and high end sneakers. It is for the sneakerheads and the collecters, only offering highly exclusive footwear at an affordable price.

Website Goals and Objectives:
- Website should be able to take customers to pay gate easily
- Website should not crash and should be linked properly
- Website should be easy to navigate
- Website should include images of inventory
- Website should have colour

Key Features and Functionality
- Can change sizes according to the orientaion of the phone or device opened on
- Has Navigation Bar
- Has functional buttons
- Has 'Add-to-cart' button


Timeline & Milestones
Development began on **15 April** and was spread across key milestones:
- **20 April – Part 1 Submission**: Initial wireframes, brand identity definition, and homepage prototype.
- **29 May – Part 2 Submission**: Product catalog integration, shopping cart functionality, and responsive design testing.
- **19 June – Final Part**: Full deployment with payment gateway, SEO optimization, and user experience refinements.


Current Website Analysis
- Strengths: Strong brand concept, clear product focus (sneakers), and recognizable name.  
- Weaknesses: Limited interactivity, lack of advanced filtering/search, and minimal storytelling about sneaker culture.  
- Opportunities: Build a vibrant e-commerce platform that not only sells sneakers but also celebrates sneaker culture through visuals, blog content, and community engagement.  


Key Features
- Responsive Design**: Works seamlessly across desktop, tablet, and mobile.  
- Product Catalog**: Organized sneaker listings with filtering by brand, size, and price.  
- Shopping Cart & Checkout**: Secure and intuitive purchase flow.  
- Payment Gateway Integration**: Support for credit cards, EFT, and mobile payments.  
- Search & Filter Tools**: Quick access to desired sneakers.  
- Customer Reviews**: Build trust and community engagement.  
- Sneaker Culture Blog**: Lifestyle content to strengthen brand identity.

Budget
The total budget allocated is *R11,240*.  
Breakdown:  
- Design & Branding: R2,500  
- Development (Frontend + Backend): R5,000  
- Content Creation (Product photos, blog posts): R2,000  
- Testing & Deployment: R1,000  
- Marketing & SEO Setup: R740  


Technical Requirements
- Languages: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript (React or Vue for interactivity).  
- Backend: Node.js or PHP with MySQL/PostgreSQL database.  
- E-commerce Framework: Shopify integration or custom-built cart system.  
- Hosting: Cloud-based hosting (AWS, Azure, or local providers).  
- Payment Gateway: PayFast or Stripe for South African market compatibility.  
- Security: SSL certificate, secure login, and encrypted transactions.  
- Browser Support: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge.  


Design & User Experience
- Colour Palette: Red, Black, White — bold, urban, and stylish.  
- Typography: Clean sans-serif font for modern readability.  
- Layout: Minimalist product grid with high-quality sneaker imagery.  
- Interactivity: Hover animations, smooth transitions, and quick-add-to-cart buttons.  
- Mobile UX: Simplified navigation, thumb-friendly buttons, and fast loading.  
- Brand Feel: Energetic, street-inspired, and culturally connected to sneaker enthusiasts.  


Website Goals
- Sales Growth: Provide a seamless shopping experience to increase conversions.  
- Brand Identity: Position DraFT.sneakers as a premium yet accessible sneaker retailer.  
- Community Engagement: Build a hub for sneaker culture through blogs and reviews.  
- Trust & Credibility: Secure payments, transparent policies, and authentic product imagery.  
- Scalability: Prepare the platform for future expansion into apparel and accessories.  

---

Conclusion
DraFT.sneakers is designed to be more than an online store — it’s a **digital sneaker hub**. With a bold design, strong technical foundation, and clear milestones, the project balances creativity, culture, and commerce within the allocated budget and timeline.
