Our Story

DraFT.sneakers was founded by M.K.B. Flatela with a vision to create an online link to premium footwear. From Nike to Puma, Reebok to Converse, DraFT.sneakers is more than just a store—it’s a platform about style, culture, and empowering you to dress as your best self.

Built on passion for sneaker culture, DraFT.sneakers connects enthusiasts with the finest collections, celebrating individuality and the art of self-expression through footwear.
